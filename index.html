THE UREKA IDEA

Our idea is to ensure that waste of all forms are not wasted and are therefored transformed via a process that will result into job creation
and economic growth whilst ensuring a cleaner enviromemt.
At Ureka, we are dedicated to ensur a cleaner society by recycling plastic waste and converting same into alternative building materials while creating wealth, reducing poverty and ensuring an Eco-friendly environment.
it’s our dream to significantly reduce the over six billion metric tons of global plastic waste, converting same into consumables.
To achieve this dream we have began interactions with necessary authorities at the state level, such as LAWMA and the Lagos state ministry of science and technology.

Objectives
• To assist in the efforts to achieve cleanliness of the State and world at large
• Encourage residents to imbibe the culture of hygiene
• Reduce the incident of indiscriminate dumping and generating zero waste
• Allow for easy end of plastic waste in our communities.
