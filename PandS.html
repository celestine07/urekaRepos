PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

SERVICES

We offer services that cut across waste recycling, waste management and consultation services.

PROTDUCTS

Our products are basically made of recycled waste materials. They are durable and highly affordable. At Ureka we conform to safety precautions to deliver cutting edge products that can compete with conventional products.
Our products include Polyketide paving stones and interlocks, polyketide flooring titles, poliketide interlocks, polyester yarns among many others.
Our polyketide paving stones and interlocks are algae proof, water resistant and acid repellent.

Objectives
• To assist in the efforts to achieve cleanliness of the State
• Encourage residents to imbibe the culture of hygiene
• Reduce the incident of indiscriminate dumping and generating zero waste
• Allow for easy end of plastic waste in our communities.
